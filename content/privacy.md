### 1. The Philosophy
We are Privacy Engineers. We believe data is a liability, not an asset.
Therefore, we collect **zero** unnecessary data.

### 2. The Stack
* **Analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics. We use server-side logs that are anonymized.
* **Cookies:** This site creates 0 cookies. We do not track you.
* **Hosting:** This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Their privacy policy applies to the infrastructure layer.

### 3. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)
Even though we don't know who you are, you have the right to ask.
Email `privacy@syntaxlegal.in` to request a data audit of our communications.
