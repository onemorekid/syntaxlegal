Technical Precision in Legal Compliance

Bridging the gap between Software Architecture and Indian Regulatory Frameworks.

Syntax Legal is a specialized consultancy operating at the intersection of technology and law. We assist organizations in navigating the complexities of the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, the Information Technology Act, and the Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam (BSA).

We do not merely interpret the law; we engineer compliance into your systems.

// Core Competencies

01. Digital Forensics & Evidence Integrity

In modern litigation, data is the primary witness.

  • Forensic Auditing: We analyze server logs, API histories, and database timestamps to establish a verifiable chain of custody.
  • Section 65B Certification: We prepare technical annexures and hash-verified reports to ensure electronic evidence is admissible in Indian courts.
  • Source Code Review: We conduct intellectual property audits to detect license violations and code theft.

02. Privacy Engineering & Architecture

Compliance cannot be solved by policy documents alone; it requires architectural enforcement.

  • Data Residency: Ensuring PII (Personally Identifiable Information) storage complies with Indian localization norms.
  • Right to Erasure: Architecting automated deletion workflows to satisfy data principal requests.
  • Security Standards: Aligning infrastructure with CERT-In guidelines and ISO 27001 standards.

03. Strategic Litigation Advisory

Guided by the Chambers of [Father-in-Law’s Name], District & Sessions Judge (Retd.)

Technical findings require legal strategy. Through our strategic partnership, we offer high-level advisory for complex litigation.

  • Scope: White Collar Crime, Cyber Litigation, and Commercial Arbitration.
  • Benefit: 30+ years of judicial experience applied to your defense strategy.
