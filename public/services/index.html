1. Digital Forensics & Evidence Packaging#

“A screenshot is not evidence.” We specialize in extracting, preserving, and certifying digital evidence for court admissibility.

  • Server Log Analysis: We audit Apache/Nginx logs to trace IP addresses and timestamps.
  • Section 65B Certification: We generate the technical certificates required under the Indian Evidence Act (BSA) to make electronic records admissible.
  • Chain of Custody: We use cryptographic hashing (SHA-256) to prove that data has not been tampered with between extraction and the courtroom.

2. Privacy Engineering (DPDP Act 2023)#

“Compliance is code, not just policy.” We help CTOs and Product Managers architect systems that are compliant by design.

  • Data Minimization Audits: Reviewing database schemas to ensure you only collect legally necessary data.
  • Right to Erasure Workflows: Designing automated pipelines to delete user data upon request (a mandatory requirement under the DPDP Act).
  • Consent Management: UX/UI reviews to ensure your “I Agree” buttons meet the legal standard of “Informed Consent.”
